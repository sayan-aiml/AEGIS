AEGIS

Agentic Decision Governance System

AEGIS is a deterministic, domain-agnostic decision governance system built on IBM watsonx Orchestrate. It is designed for high-stakes enterprise decisions where explainability, consistency, and human oversight are mandatory.

THE PROBLEM AEGIS SOLVES

Most AI systems behave like conversational assistants. While flexible, they are unsuitable for enterprise decision-making due to non-determinism, lack of accountability, and missing escalation signals.

AEGIS treats decisions as governed workflows rather than conversations.

WHAT AEGIS IS

AEGIS is not a chatbot. It executes a predefined multi-agent governance workflow and produces a single final decision per request.

One input → One governed decision → No follow-up questions → No ambiguity

MULTI-AGENT ARCHITECTURE

  • Decision Agent: Orchestrates the workflow.
  • Data Quality Agent: Validates input reliability.
  • Explainability Agent: Explains decision factors.
  • Trust & Risk Agent: Assigns confidence and escalation.
  • Human Explanation Agent: Converts output into plain language.
